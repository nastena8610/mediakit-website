Frequently Asked Questions

Before you ask.

How is FORTA different from a traditional consultancy? +
We don't produce reports and disappear. We embed with your team, build the strategy together, and then stay to implement and execute it. You get a partner who delivers results — not a consultant who bills for advice.
What size companies do you work with? +
We work primarily with startups and SMEs — from early-stage founders building their first operational structure to growing businesses that need to scale without adding unnecessary complexity.
What regions do you cover? +
We work remotely within CET business hours, covering Benelux, Nordics, United Kingdom, and the Middle East. All engagements are fully remote and flexible.
How long does a typical engagement last? +
It depends on the scope. Some projects are a one-time consultation. Others are ongoing partnerships on an hourly, daily, or project basis. We'll discuss what makes sense for your situation on the discovery call.
What does the first step look like? +
Book a free discovery call. We'll talk through your business, where you're stuck, and whether we're the right fit. If we are, you'll receive a tailored proposal within 24 hours. No pressure, no obligation.
What kind of results can I expect? +
Our clients typically see 10–12% business growth in the first engagement — driven by leaner operations, better hiring decisions, and strategies that actually get executed. Every engagement is tailored, so outcomes depend on your starting point.
